About College

NITTE Mangalore (Deemed-to-be University) was established in 2008 and is located in Mangalore. The university is accredited with an A Grade by NAAC and placed under ‘Category A’ by MHRD, GOI. The university has received a Diamond rating in the QS I-Gauge India Ratings. Some of the courses provided by the deemed to be university are accredited by NBA. The teaching hospital of NITTE University is accredited by both NABH and NABL. NITTE Mangalore is ranked 501-550 by the QS World University Rankings 2024 in Asia, 143 by the QS World University Rankings 2024 in Southern Asia, and 301–400 by the Times Higher Education Impact Rankings 2023. NITTE Mangalore, through various colleges, provides around 74 UG, PG, and various other courses to students. These courses are provided across the Physiotherapy, Speech and Audiology, Media and Communication, Biological Sciences, and various other streams. NITTE Mangalore is known for its flagship programmes such as BTech, MBBS, and others.
